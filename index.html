Scrambled States  

Instructions

1. You will be shown scrambled tiles spelling one of 50 U.S. states.  Your job is to unscramble them.

2. When typing your responses, spelling counts but not capitalization.

3. A blank tile mean there's a space in the name.  The above example spells NORTH DAKOTA.

4. You have 60 seconds to get as many states as possible.

Let's Start!  60 second timer...
